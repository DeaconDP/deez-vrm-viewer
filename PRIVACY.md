# Deez VRM Viewer — Privacy Notice

**Version 1.0 · Effective 3 July 2026**

This notice explains how Dale Deacon, trading as Worldbuild.io and deac.online
(“Provider”, “we”, “us”), handles information in Deez VRM Viewer (the “App”).

## The short version

The current App is local-first. It has no Provider-operated account, analytics,
advertising, telemetry, tracking pixel, or cloud upload. Model files selected
from your device are processed in your browser and are not intentionally sent
to us. The App cannot promise that other parties—such as a remote model host,
website host, browser vendor, operating system, network administrator, or app
distribution platform—collect nothing.

## Information processed on your device

The App processes the model files and URLs you choose, including their names,
metadata, textures, geometry, licence information, and any personal information
embedded in them. This processing occurs transiently in browser memory so the
App can render and inspect the model. Files are not intentionally uploaded to
or stored by the Provider. Closing/reloading the App releases transient data,
subject to ordinary browser and operating-system behaviour.

The App may store only the following on your device:

- the version/date of the Terms you accepted; and
- ordinary service-worker/cache data needed for offline PWA operation.

You can clear this data using your browser’s site-data controls or by removing
the installed PWA. Screenshots are saved only when and where you direct.

## Public URLs and third parties

When you enter a public model URL, your browser makes a direct request to that
remote server. The server may receive your IP address, user-agent or browser
details, request time, referrer information (depending on browser policy), and
the requested URL. The remote operator determines why and how it handles that
data. Review its privacy policy before proceeding. The Provider does not
receive that request merely because you used the App.

If you access a hosted version of the App, its hosting and network providers
may automatically process routine HTTP/security logs. If a distributor,
browser, operating system, or device store supplies the App, that party may
independently process downloads, updates, diagnostics, purchases, or device
information under its own privacy notice. Links to external sites take you out
of the App.

## Purposes and legal bases

Local processing is initiated by you and necessary to provide the viewer
functionality you request. Any limited information we receive when you contact
us is used to respond, administer the App, protect legal rights and security,
and comply with law. Depending on the context, the basis is performance of a
request or contract, our legitimate interests, consent, or legal obligation.

We do not sell personal information, use it for behavioural advertising,
profile App users, or make decisions producing legal or similarly significant
effects. We do not knowingly collect children’s personal information through
the App. A parent or guardian should supervise a minor’s use.

## Contact messages, retention, and sharing

If you contact us outside the App, we receive the details and content you send.
Do not send sensitive information or model files unless requested. We retain
correspondence only as reasonably needed to respond, keep appropriate business
and legal records, resolve disputes, and meet legal obligations. We may share
it with service providers acting for us, professional advisers, authorities
when lawfully required, or a successor in a business transfer. We do not sell
it. Providers may process data in other countries subject to applicable
safeguards.

## Security

Local processing reduces—but does not eliminate—risk. No software, device, or
transmission is completely secure. Keep your browser and operating system
updated, obtain models only from sources you trust, and avoid remote URLs that
contain secret tokens or personal information.

## Your rights

Subject to applicable law, including South Africa’s Protection of Personal
Information Act 4 of 2013 (POPIA), you may ask whether we hold personal
information about you and request access, correction, deletion, restriction,
or objection. Because the App sends us no user identifier or model file, we
generally cannot identify or retrieve local App data; clear it on your device.
We may need to verify a request and may retain information where law permits.

You may complain to the [Information Regulator (South Africa)](https://inforegulator.org.za/)
or your local data-protection authority. We encourage you to contact us first.

## Changes and contact

We will update the version/date when this notice materially changes and will
seek renewed acknowledgement where required. For privacy questions or rights
requests, use the contact details at [worldbuild.io](https://worldbuild.io/) or
[deac.online](https://deac.online/).
